====== 25. Sheexaanu Yesuusa Jifisi ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-25-01.jpg}}

Yesuusa cuuame ka”anni hee’reennanni Qullaawu Ayyaani halallitte gabba haa’re ha’risi. Hakkiicho shoyilloo barranna shoyilloo hashsha qatumi. Hakkunni Gedensaanni Sheexaanu daye cubbo loosiisa hede Yesuusa jifisi.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-25-02.jpg}}

Sheexaanu, <> yee Yesuusa jifisi.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-25-03.jpg}}

Yesuusi, << yine borreessinoonni!>> yee dawarisi.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-25-04.jpg}}

Hakkunni gedensaanni Sheexaanu Yesuusa Qullaawu Maxaafi birxe fushshe, <<Ati Maganu Beettootiro, atenee kawiinni eela qolte tugi; yine borreessinoonnina>> yii.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-25-05.jpg}}

Yesuusi kayinni Sheexaaneho Maganu qaale qummi asse qolisi. <<Maganu qaali manna, yee hajajanno>> yii.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-25-06.jpg}}

Hakkunni gedensaanni Sheexaanu baattote gashshuwanna ayirrinyensa baala leellishe, <> yii.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-25-07.jpg}}

Yesuusi, <<Ati Sheexaane, kai kawiinni! Maganu qaali manna, yee hajajanno>> yii.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-25-08.jpg}}

Yesuusi Sheexaanu jifonni diqeelamino; kunnira Sheexaanu yannate geeshsha Isiwiinni agure ha’rino. Hakkunni gedensaanni sokkaasine dagge soqqantinosi.

//Qullaawu Maxaafi Dhagge:- Maatewoosi 4:1-11; Maarqoosi 1:12-13; Luqaasi 4:1-13//
