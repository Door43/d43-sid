====== 39. Yesuusa Yoote Shiqishshusi ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-39-01.jpg}}

Xa yanna hashshu taalooteeti. Qeesootu Roorrichi xa’masira gaaddo Yesuusa haadhe minisira massitusi. Pheexiroosi xeerti’re ha’runsanni no. Yesuusa qeesootu Roorrichi minira eessitusita, Pheexiroosi gobbaanni gate giira giwaa’ranni no.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-39-02.jpg}}

Mine Ayihudete sooreeyye Yesuusa yoote shiqishshino. Beebba coyidhannore kaphu naqaashshe haadhe abbitu. Ikkollana, qaalinsa mimmitu ledo dixaadino; Kunnira, Ayihudete sooreeyye Yesuusi sao assinoronna hooginoro buuxate qarrantu.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-39-03.jpg}}

Goofimarchoho, Qeesootu Roorrichi Yesuusa qixxi asse lae, <> yiisi.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-39-04.jpg}}

Yesuusi, <> yiisi. Qeesootu Roorrichi hanqe uddanosi dare woloota amma’note sooreeyye, <<Wole Naqaashshe dihasiissanno! yaanna macciishshitinoonni. Xa mayitinanni?>> yee canci.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-39-05.jpg}}

Ayihudete sooreeyye baalunku, <> yite Qeesootu Roorrichira dawartu. Hakkunni gedensaanni albasi fugge, anja tuffusi; gantusi; qacifattusi.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-39-06.jpg}}

Pheexiroosi gobbaanni galaalchanni hee’reenna mine soqqantanno beetto la’e, <> yitusi. Pheexiroosi ise yitinota diammanino. Hakkunni gedensaanni wole soqqamaanchono dagge hattonni yitinosi; Pheexiroosi xaano diammanino. Goofimarchoho mannu, <> yitu.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-39-07.jpg}}

Hakkunni gedensaanni Pheexiroosi, <> yee xaari. Hakko mereero lukkichu boroodi; Yesuusi caaqqi yee Pheexiroosi lai.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-39-08.jpg}}

Pheexiroosi ha’re hururame wi’li. Tenne giddo Yesuusa sayise uyi Yihudi Ayihudete sooreeyye Yesuusi reyanno gede yoo yitinota afi. Dadillu egemmeennasi ha’re qalxi’re reyi.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-39-09.jpg}}

Layinki soodo guto Ayihudete sooreeyye Yesuusa haadhe Roomu gashshaanchiwa massitu. Philaaxoosi sao noosi yee reyanno gede assanno yite heddino. Philaaxoosi Yesuusa, <> yee xa’misi.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-39-10.jpg}}

Yesuusi daware, <> yii. Philaaxoosi qole, <> yii.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-39-11.jpg}}

Philaaxoosi Yesuusi ledo hasaawihu gedensaanni mannunniwa daye, <> yii. Ayihudete sooreeyye kayinni, <> yitanni cancitu. Philaaxoosi kayinni, <> yee dawarinsa. Insa kayinni ledde cancitu. Hakkunni gedensaanni Philaaxoosi sayikkimeeshsho, <> yii.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-39-12.jpg}}

Philaaxoosi mannu cancamanno yee waajjino; kunnira Yesuusa suttanno gede Gaaddo hajaji. Roomu gaaddo Yesuusa gante duume uddanno uddisiissinosi; utu ba’latto umoho wortinosi. Hakkunni gedensaanni, <> yitanni qacifattusi.

//Qullaawu Maxaafi Dhagge:- Maatewoosi 26:57-27:26; Maarqoosi 14:53-15; 15; Luqaasi 22:54-23:25; Yohaannisi 18:12-19:16//
