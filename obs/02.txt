====== 2. Cubbu Kalqete Giddora Ei ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-01.jpg}}

Addaaminna galtesi Maganu seekkinonsawa haqqe noowa heedhu waro hagiirraammaho. Lamunku mulluwu no; ikkollana mllootu hee’ransa saalfattanno gede diassitinonsa. Hakkuno cubbu kalqete nookkihuraati. Insano haqqete giddo ha’rantanni Maganu ledo hasaabbanni heedhino.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-02.jpg}}

Ikkollana xagaraamu hamashshi haqqete giddo no. Manchono, <> yiise.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-03.jpg}}

Manchono, <<Maganu bushanna dancha egensiissanno haqqicho agurranna haqqe baalate laalo ita dandiineemmota kulinonke. yiinonke>> yitu.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-04.jpg}}

Hamashshu qole, <> yii.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-05.jpg}}

Mancho haqqichote laalo itate dancha ikkitinota illete biiffinotanna egenno afi’rate halchishshannota ikkitinota la’e haadhe ittu. Ayiddaanniserano uyitu. Isino haqqichote laalo iti.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-06.jpg}}

Hakkawoyintenni illensa fa’nitu. Mullootu nootano buuxxu; daro uddanote gede assite suffe xeeda diphate wo’naaltu.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-07.jpg}}

Manchonna minaannise haqqete giddo ha’rantanni noota Maganu huuro macciishshite lamunku albisinni maaxantu. Maganuno Addaami woshshe <> yiisi. Addaamino, <> yee dawari.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-08.jpg}}

Maganuno, <> yiisi. Addaamino, <> yii. Maganuno mancho <> yiise. Iseno, <> yitu.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-09.jpg}}

Maganu hamashsho, <> yii.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-10.jpg}}

Maganu manchono, <> yiise.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-11.jpg}}

Maganu Addaamino, <> yiisi. Addaami ayiddaamasi lubbotenni noo baalira amate yee su’ma, <> yee fushshise. Hatto yaa lubbo uyitanno yaate. Maganu saadate goga seekke Addaaminna Heewani uddisiisinsa.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-12.jpg}}

Maganu mannu danchanna busha bade afatenni ninke giddonni mittu gedeeha ikkino; kunni ka’a hiixate angasinni heeshshote haqqicho laalo tayise itannokki gede hegerano hee’rannokki gede Addaaminna Heewani biifado haqqe giddonni fushshinsa. Hakkunni gedensaanni heeshshote haqqichowa massitanno doogo agaranno gedenna tenne haqqicho laalono tayise itannokki gede wolqaataamma sokkaasine laalote haqqewa wori.
