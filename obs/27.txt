====== 27. Danchu Samaarichi Dhagge ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-27-01.jpg}}

Mitto barra mittu seeru fullahi Ayihudichu Yesuusa fonqolara kae daye, <> yiisi. Yesuusi qole, Yesuusi qole, <> yii.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-27-02.jpg}}

Seeru fullahi, <<Maganu Seeri, yaanno>> yii. Yesuusi qole, <> yiisi.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-27-03.jpg}}

Seeru fullahi kayinni keeraancho ikkinota buuxi’rara hasi’re, <> yee xa’mi.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-27-04.jpg}}

Yesuusi seeru fullahira dhaggeessanni, <<Mittu Ayihudichi Yerusaalametenni kae Iyyarko ha’ri.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-27-05.jpg}}

Manchu ha’ranni hee’reenna addifulootu dagge si’usi. Noosire baala adhite ganatenni shite dalaginoha agurte hadhino.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-27-06.jpg}}

Hakkunni gedensaanni hakkawoyintenni mittu Ayihudichu qeesichi doogo hajamanni hakkaanni hige saino. Kuni amma’note sooreessi gannoonni mancho lae shawwu yee sae ha’rino. Kaa’lo hasi’re noo mancho afanni hee’re agure doogosi ha’rino.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-27-07.jpg}}

Hakkunni gedensaanni lowo geeshshano keeshshikkinni, mittu Lewaawichu kae dayi. (Lewaawootu Ayihudete giddonni Qullaawa Mine qeesoota kaa’late doorantino ga’reeti.) Lewaawichuno mulqi’noonnihanna gannoonni mancho afanni hee’re shawwu yee wole doogo hige ha’rino.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-27-08.jpg}}

Hattenne doogo hige ha’rinohu mittu Samaarichaho. (Samaarootu wole gobba ledo adhantino Ayihudootaati. Kunnira Ayihudootu insa ledo dibaxantanno.) Ikkollana Samaarichu gannoonni Ayihudicha afi yannara lowo geeshsha mararinosi. Kunnira shiqqi yee kaa’le madano usurinosi.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-27-09.jpg}}

Hakkunni gedensaanni Samaarichu mancho duhe harrichisi aana wore wosinu galannowa masse towatinosi.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-27-10.jpg}}

Soodita Samaarichu doogosi hajama hasi’rino. Wosinu mini annira shiima woxe ee, yiino>> yiisi.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-27-11.jpg}}

Hakkunni gedensaanni Yessusi seeru fullahira, <> yee xa’misi. Isino, <> yee dawari. Yesuusino, <> yee kulisi.

//Qullaawu Maxaafi Dhagge:- Luqaasi 10:25-37//
