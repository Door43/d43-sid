====== 6. Maganu Yisihaaqira Galte Qixxeessisi ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-06-01.jpg}}

Abirihaami lowo geeshsha geedhi yannara Yisihaaqi qeedhicha ikki. Kunnira Abirihaami soqqamaanosi giddonni mitto ayiddisi gobba ha’re Yisihaaqira seenne abbannosi gede soyi.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-06-02.jpg}}

Abirihaami ayiddi hee’ranno gobba seeda doogo gedensaanni mare iilli. Maganu sonkoonniha Abirihaami soqqamaancho Ribiqanni xaadisisi. Ribiqa Abirihaami rodiihu beettu beetti ilinote.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-06-03.jpg}}

Ribiqa ayiddese agurte soqqamaanchu ledo Yisihaaqi minira hadhara sumuu yitu. Marte iillitanni heedheenninni Yisihaaqi galte ikkitu.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-06-04.jpg}}

Seedu diri gedensaanni Abirihaami reenna Maganu Abirihaami ledo eino > gondooro baala Yisihaaqira sayisi. Maganu Abirihaamira kiirre > fullannikki sirchi hee’rannosita balaxe coyi’rino. Ikkollana Yisihaaqi > galte Ribiqa ooso ila didandiitino.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-06-05.jpg}}

Yisihaaqi Ribiqara Magano huucceennase lakkuwa godobbanno gede assise. Lamenti ooso Ribiqa otootto giddo heedhe mimmitinsa ledo sharrantino. Ribiqano otoottose giddo ikkanni noorira Magano xa’midhino.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-06-06.jpg}}
Maganuno Ribiqara, <<Otoottokki giddo noori lamente oosokki giddonni lame gosa fultanno. Insa mimmitu ledo sharrantanno. Bayiru maa’neho soqqamanno>> yiise. 

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-06-07.jpg}}

Ribiqa ooso ilantu woyite bayiru beetti biishshahonna baancaamoho. Su’mano, <> yite fushshitusi. Maa’nu beetti Eesawu ginde amade fulino. Isira su’ma, <> yite fushshitinosi.

Qullaawu Maxaafi Dhagge:- Kalaqama 24-25; 26.
