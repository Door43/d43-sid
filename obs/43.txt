====== 43. Beetekiristaanete Xintama ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-43-01.jpg}}

Yesuusi gordo higihu gedensaanni, rosaano Yesuusi hajajinonsa garinni Yerusaalamete agartu. Hakkiicho noo ammanaano murtukkinni huucci’rate gamba yitanno.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-43-02.jpg}}

Ayihude diru dirunkunni Agure Sa”ate Ayyaanira 50 barri gedensaanni Phenxeqosxete yinanni barra ayirrissanno. Phenxeqosxe Ayihude Gide Gamba Assi’nanni Ayyaana ayirrisidhanno ayyaanaati. Phenxeqosxete Ayyaana mitteenni ayirrisate Ayihude kalqe baalatenni dagganno. Konne diro Yesuusi gordo higihunni mitte lamala gedensaanni Phenxeqosxete barri dayi.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-43-03.jpg}}

Ammanaano baalanti mitteenni gamba yite heedheenna, hedeweelcho huuro labbannoti wolqaataame bubbe insa noo mine wo’mitu. Hakkunni gedensaanni ammanaasine baalante aana giirate buwano lawannori leelli. Baalunkura Qullaawu Ayyaani wo’meenna wolu afiinni coyi’ra hanaffu.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-43-04.jpg}}

Yerusaalamete noo manni tenne huuro macciishshe, ikkanni noo coye la”ate haammate dayi. Ammanaano Maganunniha dhaggete looso kultanni macciishshino manni konne coye uminsa afiinni macciishsha danddino daafira dhaggeeffati.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-43-05.jpg}}

Mitu mitu manni, <> yee bushiishinonsa. Ikkollana Pheexiroosi kae uurre, <<Macciishshe”e! Kuni manni didimbino! Maganu Himanaanchu Iyyueeli widoonni qole yiino himani wo’manni no.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-43-06.jpg}}

Israeelete manna, ki’ne la’inoonninna albaannino affinoonninte gede Yesuusi Maganu wolqanni haammata dhaggenna maalale loosino. Ki’ne kayino Iso suttinoonni!

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-43-07.jpg}}

Yesuusi reyiro nafa Maganu reyotenni kayisinosi. Kuni coyi ikkinohu, yinoonni himani wo’manno gedeeti. Maganu Yesuusa heeshshonniha asse kayisasira ninke naqaashshete.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-43-08.jpg}}

Yesuusi xa Maganu Annu qiniiteenni ayirre no. Qoleno Yesuusi uyino hexxo Qullaawa Ayyaana soyino. Xa la’inannirenna macciishshitinanni heedhinoonnire Qullaawu Ayyaani assanni no.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-43-09.jpg}}

Ki’ne konne Yesuusa suttinoonni. Ikkollana Maganu Yesuusa Mootichanna Buuramino Moote assinosita affe!>> yiinsa.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-43-10.jpg}}

Pheexiroosi yiinore macciishshiture coyi’rino coyi lowo geeshsha milli assinonsa. Kunnira Pheexiroosinna rosaano, <> yite xa’mitu.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-43-11.jpg}}

Pheexiroosi, <> yiinsa.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-43-12.jpg}}

3000 ikkanno manni Pheexiroosi coyi’rino coye ammane Yesuusi rosaano ikki. Kuni manni cuuame Yerusaalamete Beetekiristaane miila ikki.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-43-13.jpg}}

Rosaano uurrissannokkiha Soqqamaasine rosiissannore macciishshitu. Mitteenni waartu. Mitteenni ittu. Mimmitoho huuccitu. Mitteenni Magano galaxxitu; noonsare baala mitteenni beeqqitu. Mannu baalunku insa daafira danchumma hedanno. Barru barrunkunni lowo manni ammani.

//Qullaawu Maxaafi Dhagge:- Soqqamaasinete Looso 2//
