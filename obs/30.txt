====== 30. Yesuusi Onte Kume Manna Itisi ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-30-01.jpg}}

Yesuusi batinyu addi addi qachira kultanno gedenna manna rosiissanno gede Soqqamaasinesi soyi. Insa Yesuusi noowa higgu yannara, assitinore kultusi. Hakkunni gedensaanni Yesuusi baara tayisse ga’labbicha darga ledosi hadhanno gedenna shiima geeshsha fooliishshidhanno gede kulinsa. Kunnira yowolote giddora e’e baara widoonni fultu.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-30-02.jpg}}

Ikkollana yowolotenni hadhanni heedheenna Yesuusanna rosaanosi afinohu lowo manni no. Kuni manni insa balaxe sa”ara baaru gamira dodi. Kunnira Yesuusinna rosaanosi widoonni fultanno woyite, lowo manna balaxe sae hakkiicho insa agare no.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-30-03.jpg}}

Mannu meentonna ooso kiirrikkinni 5000 sa”anno labballooti. Yesuusi manna lowo geeshsha marari. Allaalaanchu nookkiha ballehino ge’reewi gede ikke no. Kunnira rosiisi kae giddonsa noo dhiwamaanono hursinsa.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-30-04.jpg}}

Hakko hashsha rosaanosi, <> yite kultusi.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-30-05.jpg}}

Yesuusi kayinni rosaanosi, <> yii. Insa qolte, <> yite dawartu.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-30-06.jpg}}

Yesuusi, <> yee rosaanosira kuli.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-30-07.jpg}}

Yesuusi onte buddeenchonna lame qulxu’micho haa’re gordo hige lae, sagalete daafira Magano huucci.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-30-08.jpg}}

Hakkunni gedensaanni Yesuusi buddeenanna qulxu’micho haa’re hinkotenni qase rosaanosi mannaho uyitanno gede uyi. Rosaanosi sagale mannaho iillishshu; sagale horonta digooffino! Mannu baalunku ite duuwi.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-30-09.jpg}}

Rosaano gatino giggilo gamba assitu; gamba assitinohu tonaa lame soro kai! Kageeshsha baala ka’u sagale onte buddeenchootinna lame qulxu’michooti.

//Qullaawu Maxaafi Dhagge:- Maatewoosi 14:13-21; Maarqoosi 6:31-44; Luqaasi 9:10-17; Yohaannisi 6:5-15//
