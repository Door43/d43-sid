====== 24. Yohaannisi Yesuusa Cuui ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-24-01.jpg}}

Zakkariyaasinna Elsaabeexi beetti Yohaannisi lophe himanaancho ikki. Halallitte gobbara hee’rino; godu malawonna kanchafare saga’lino; gaalu ordanni gannoonni imaana uddi’rino.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-24-02.jpg}}

Lowo manni Yohaannisi yaannore macciishshate halallira dayi. Isi, <> yee kulino.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-24-03.jpg}}

Mannu Yohaannisi sokka macciishshe, rooru manni cubbonsa ifaaxxi; Yohaannisi cuuinsa. Haammatu amma’note sooreeyyeno Yohaannisi cuuansara daggu; ikkollana diaagintaabbino woy cubbo di”ifaxxitino.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-24-04.jpg}}

Yohaannisi amma’note sooreeyyera, <> yiinsa. Himanaano Yohaannisi, << yiino>> yite himantino himana wonshi.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-24-05.jpg}}

Mite mite Ayihude Yohaannisi Buuramino Moote ikkinoronna hooginoro xa’mitino. Yohaannisi, <<Ani Buuramino Moote di”ikkoommo; ane gedensaanni kayinni mittu daannohu no. Isi rooreho. Ani koattesi suncho nafa tirate di”ikkoommoho.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-24-06.jpg}}

Aane noo barra Yesuusi cuuasira Yohaannisiwa kae dayi. Yohaannisi iso afe, <> yii.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-24-07.jpg}}

Yohaannisi Yesuusa, <> yii. Yesuusi kayinni, <> yii. Kunnira Yesuusi horonta cubbo loosinokkiha ikkirono Yohaannisi Yesuusa cuuinosi.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-24-08.jpg}}

Yesuusi cuuamihu gedensaanni wayi giddonni fuli; Maganu Ayyaani lembooli daninni leellanni dirre aanasi ofolli. Hakko mereero Maganu huuro gordunni, <> yitanni daggu.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-24-09.jpg}}

//Maganu Yohaannisira, <> yee coyi’rinosi. Mittu Magani callu no. Ikkollana Yohaannisi Yesuusa cuui yannara, Maganu Annu coyi’ranna macciishshi. Magano Beetto afino. Hakku Yesuusaati. Qullaawa Ayyaanano laino. //Qullaawu Maxaafi Dhagge:- Maatewoosi 3; Maarqoosi 1:9-11; Luqaasi 3:1-23////
