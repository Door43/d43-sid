====== 7. Maganu Yaiqoobi Maassi’risi ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-07-01.jpg}}

Ooso lophituhu gedensaanni Yaiqoobi amate ledo mine hosa baxannoha ikki. Eesawu kayinni ugaaxe baxanno. Ribiqa Yaiqoobi baxxanno; Yisihaaqi kayinni Eesawu baxanno.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-07-02.jpg}}

Mitto barra Eesawu ugaaxetewiinni higanno woyite hudi’re no. Eesawu Yaiqoobi, <> yiisi. Yaiqoobi kayinni <> yee dawarisi. Kunnira Eesawu bayirreenyasi Yaiqoobira sayise uyi. Yaiqoobino qixxeessi’rino sagale aaninni uyinosi.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-07-03.jpg}}

Yisihaaqi maasso Eesawura aa hasi’rino. Ikkollana hakkunnira albaanni Ribiqanna Yaiqoobi Yisihaaqi dogginosi. Yaiqoobi Eesawu lawe Yisihaaqiwa shiqino. Yisihaaqi geedhino daafira illeno afa gibbe noosi. Kunnira Yaiqoobi Eesawu imaana uddi’re meichu goga goowahonna angate xaaxi’re shiqe Yisihaaqi dogino.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-07-04.jpg}}

Yaiqoobi Yisihaaqiwa daye, <> yii. Yisihaaqi meichu orda amammade lae imaanu fooleno ulate Eesawu ikkanno yee hede maassi’rinosi.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-07-05.jpg}}

Yaiqoobi bayirreenyu doyichonna maasso doge adhinosi. Eesawu Yaiqoobi giwisi. Anninsa reyihu gedensaanni shaasira hasi’rino.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-07-06.jpg}}

Ribiqa kayinni Eesawu hedinore macciishshitu. Kunnira isenna Yisihaaqi ha’re  
Fiixisiwa hee’ranno gede Yaiqoobi xeerto gobba ha’rissu


{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-07-07.jpg}}

Yaiqoobi seeda diro Ribiqa ayiddiwa hee’ri. Hatte yannara galte kalaqi’re 12 labballonna 1 meya beetto ili. Maganu lowo geeshsha duroonsisi.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-07-08.jpg}}

Yaiqoobi Kana”aani gobbara 20 diro hee’rihu gedensaanni maatesi, soqqamaanosinna saadasi baala adhe qae higara kai.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-07-09.jpg}}

Eesawu xaano shaaera malanni no yee hedino daafira waajji. Kunnira afansha ikkitannota lowo saada alba qole Eesawura soyi. Saada abbitinori Yaiqoobi soqqamaano Eesawu, <> yitusi.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-07-10.jpg}}

Eesawu kayinni balaxe Yaiqoobira higinosi daafira galagale xaadate hagiirraamoho. Yaiqoobi Kana”aani gobbara keere hee’rino. Yisihaaqino reyino. Yaiqoobinna Eesawu madaartusi. Maganu Abirihaamira uyino hexxo xa Yisihaaqi beettira Yaiqoobira sa’u.

Qullaawu Maxaafi Dhagge:- Kalaqama 25:27-33:20.
