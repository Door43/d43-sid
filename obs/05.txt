====== 5. Hexxote Beetto ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-05-01.jpg}}

Tonnu diri gedensaanni Abiraaminna Soora marte Kana”aani iillitu. Hatte yanna geeshsha ila dinonsa. Kunnira Abiraami galte Soora, <> yitu.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-05-02.jpg}}

Kunnira Abiraami Aggaara adhi. Aggaara labbaa beetto iltu. Abiraami, <> yee su’ma fushshisi. Ikkollana Soora Aggaara hinaassu; Isimaeelira tonaa sase diro ikkisita Maganu Abiraami,

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-05-03.jpg}}

<> yiisi. Abiraami baattote aana gombisami. Maganuno Abiraami, <> yiisi.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-05-04.jpg}}

Maganu, <<Galtekki Soora labbaa beetto iltanno; isi hexxote beetto ikkanno. Su’mano, yite woshshattosi. Gondooro’ya isi ledo e”eemmo>> yii. Lowo gosa ikkanno. Isimaeelino lowo gosa ikkanno. Ikkollana gondoori’ya Yisihaaqi ledo ikkanno>> yii. Maganu Abiraami su’ma, yee soorrisi. Hatto yaa <> yaate. Abirihaami Soora su’mano, yee soorrise. Hatto yaa <> yaate.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-05-05.jpg}}

Hakko barranni Abirihaami minesi heedhanno labballo baala barcimishiishi. Diru gedensaanni Abirihaamira 100 diro ikkisita Saara 99 dirisenni Abirihaamira labbaa beetto iltusi. Maganu yiinonsante gede su’ma, <> yite fushshitusi.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-05-06.jpg}}

Yisihaaqi wedellicha ikki yannara Maganu Abirihaami amma’na fonqolara yee, <> yiisi. Xaano Abirihaami Maganoho hajajame beettosi kakkalote qixxeessi.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-05-07.jpg}}

Abirihaaminna Yisihaaqi kakkalo shiqinshanniwa ha’ra hanaffu. Yisihaaqi annasi፣ <> yee xa’mi. Abirihaamino, <> yee dawarinosi.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-05-08.jpg}}

Kakkalo shiqinshanniwa iillituta Abirihaami beettosi Yisihaaqi usure kakkalote darga haawiisse wori. Abirihaami beettosi gorrara billawa haa’re gotti assi; Maganu, <> yiisi.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-05-09.jpg}}

Abirihaami mulesi citu giddo usurame noo ge’recho afi. Maganu Yisihaaqi darga kakkalote ikkanno ge’recho qixxeessino yaate. Abirihaamino hagiirrunni ge’recho kakkali.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-05-10.jpg}}

Maganu Abirihaamira, <<Mittichu beettikki nafa gatikkinni noohere baala aattoera owaatoottona maassi’reemmohe gede coyi’roommo. Sirchikki gordu beeddahenni roore bati’ranno. Anera hajajamoottoe daafira baattote aana noo manni baalu atenni maassamanno.

//Qullaawu Maxaafi Dhagge:- Kalaqama 16-22//
