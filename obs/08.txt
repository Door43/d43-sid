====== 8. Maganu Yooseefinna Ayiddesi Gatisi ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-08-01.jpg}}

Anninsa Yaiqoobi Yooseefi roduuwisi baaluwiinni roorse baxannosi daafiranna roorrichansa ikkannota leellishshanno haqee haaqii’rino daafira roduuwisi gibbinosi. Yooseefi roduuwisiwa daanno woyite haqaqqaalte daddalaasinete hirtinosi.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-08-02.jpg}}

Lowo diri gedensaanni Yaiqoobi geedhe hee’re saadasi allaaltannori roduuwisi noo gara la”anno gede beettosi Yooseefi fushshe soyino.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-08-03.jpg}}

Yooseefi roduuwi qaera daggara albaanni Yooseefi imaana daddarte meichu mundeera cuu’ino. Hakkunni gedensaanni mundeete cuu’inoha Yooseefi imaana anninsara leellishshino. Isino Yooseefi moyichu shiino yee hedino. Yaiqoobi lowo geeshsha manchitino.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-08-04.jpg}}

Daddalaasine Yooseefi Gibitse haadhe hadhino. Hatte yannara jawa gobba Gibitsenniti Abbayi Wayi mule heedhanno. Daddalaasine Yooseefi borojjicho assite dureessaho gashshootu roorrichira hirtinosi. Yooseefi biilisira dancha gede soqqamino. Maganuno Yooseefi maassi’rinosi.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-08-05.jpg}}

Biilisi galte Yooseefi ledo goxate wo’naaltino. Yooseefi kayinni Maganu albaanni cubbo dilooseemmo yee giwino. Ise aarantino daafira beebbatenni bushiishsheennasi amande usuru mine tungoonnisi. Usuru mine hee’rirono ammanaminosi daafira Maganu maassi’rinosi.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-08-06.jpg}}

Yooseefi fayyaaleessa nafa ikkiro lamu diri gedensaannino usuru mineno no. Mitto hashsha Gibitsootu yitannohu Feriooni wodahaminota lame haaqee haaqi’rino. Amaalaasinesi giddonni mittuno haaqee tira didandiino.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-08-07.jpg}}

Maganu Yooseefira haaqee tirate hayyo uyinosi. Kunnira Feriooni Yooseefi usuru mininni fule isiwa daanno gede assisi. Yooseefi haaqee tire, <> yii.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-08-08.jpg}}

Feriooni Yooseefi coye dhaggeeffati; Gibitsete baatto baalate isinni aane layinki gashshaancho ikkanno gede shoomisi.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-08-09.jpg}}

Yooseefi mannaho lamalu duuwu diriha lowo gide gootaraho gamba assanno kulinsa. Lamalu hudete diri dayita Yooseefi gootaru giddonni gide hiranna mannu ikkitanno sagale afi’ri.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-08-10.jpg}}

Hudete qarri Gibitsete calla ikkikkinni Yaiqoobinna maatesi heedhannotera Kana”aanino gobbarano no.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-08-11.jpg}}

Yaiqoobi oososi gide hidhitanno gede Gibitse soyinsa. Yooseefi roduuwi gide hidhate hadhe albasiinni uurritu yannara Yooseefi ikkinota dihuwattino. Yooseefi kayinni afinonsa.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-08-12.jpg}}

Yooseefi roduuwasi fonqolinsahu gedensaanni woyyaabbino gedeenni afara yee, <> yiinonsa.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-08-13.jpg}}

Yooseefi roduuwi gobbansa higge Yooseefi lubbotenni noota anninsa Yaiqoobira kulteennasi lowo geeshsha hagiidhino.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-08-14.jpg}}

Yaiqoobi geedhirono, maatesi baalante ledo Gibitse ha’reenna hakkiicho heedhino. Yaiqoobi reyara albaanni oososi mitto mittonka maassi’rinonsa.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-08-15.jpg}}

Maganu Abirihaamira uyinoti gondooru hexxo Yisihaaqira sa’ino. Hakkunni gedensaanni Yaiqoobira, aanteno tonaa lamunku Yaiqoobi oosoranna maatensara sa’ino. Tonaa lamente ooso sirchi tona lame ga’re ikkino.

//Qullaawu Maxaafi Dhagge:- Kalaqama 37-50//
