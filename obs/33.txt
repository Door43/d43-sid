====== 33. Baatto Loosi’rannohu Dhagge ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-33-01.jpg}}

Mitto barra Yesuusi Baaru gama lowo manna rosiisi. Yesuusi rosiisate dargu tola hooginohura wayi gama yowolote giddora e”e ofolla geeshsha macciishshate Lowo manni dayi. Isino manna yowolote aana ofolle rosiisi.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-33-02.jpg}}

Yesuusi, <<Mittu baatto loosi’rannohu wixa wixi’rara fuli. Wixa wixanna mitu mitu Doogote gama uwi; cea dagge hakkonne wixa gudisse ittu.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-33-03.jpg}}

Wolu wixi bushshu qawaaxxisinokkiwa carete aana uwi; ikkollana rumushshu baattote giddora raqqe ea didandiino. Arrishsho flute iibbitanno woyite, mu’ru qoore kae mooli.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-33-04.jpg}}

Wolu wixino utu giddo uwi. Kuni wixi mu’rino; ikkollana utu qalxe amadinosi. Kunnira utu giddo mu’rinohuno guma laala dindandiino.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-33-05.jpg}}

Wolu wixi kayinni dancha baatto aana uwino. Kuni wixi mu’reenna mittu 30, mittu

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-33-06.jpg}}

, mittu 100 guma laalino. Macca noohu macciishsho!>> yii.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-33-07.jpg}}

Tini dhagge rosaanote leella gibbinonsa. Kunnira Yesuusi, <<Wixu Maganu qaaleeti. Doogote gami Maganu qaale macciishshanno manchooti; ikkollana diwodanchanno; Daawuloosi qaale giddosinni adhanno.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-33-08.jpg}}

Caraame baatto Maganu qaale macciishshe hagiirrunni adhanno manchooti. Ikkollana qarru woy xiiwama daggannosi woyite ammanasi aguranno.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-33-09.jpg}}

Utaame baatto Maganu qaale macciishshanno manchooti; ikkollana gedensaanni tenne gobba hedo, jironna hagiirru Maganoho noosi baxille qalxanno. Kunninni kainohunni, macciishshino coyinni guma laala hooganno.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-33-10.jpg}}

Dancha baatto kayinni Maganu qaale macciishshe, ammanannonna guma laalanno manchooti.

//Qullaawu Maxaafi Dhagge:- Maatewoosi 13:1-8, 18-23; Maarqoosi 4:1-8, 13-20; 15፡፡ Luqaasi 8:4-15//
