====== 11. Agure Sa”ate Ayyaana ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-11-01.jpg}}

Israeeloota agurannokkiha ikkiro mannunniha umo ilaminohanna saadanniha umo qalaminoha baala shaannota kule Feriooni qorophisiisino. Feriooni konne coyeno macciishshe ammananna Maganohono hajajama giwi.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-11-02.jpg}}

Maganu Iso ammaninosi baalita umi ila gatisate doogo qixxeessino. Mitte mittenti maate muso afi’rinokki wilii’licho doorte gorridhino.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-11-03.jpg}}

Maganu Israeelootaho wilii’lichu mundee mininsa waalchira ga’nitanno gede, maalano du’rishshe shiishikkinni gannoonni timi ledo rakke ittanno gede kulinonsa. Ittanno woyiteno Gibitsetenni fulate qixxaabbanno gede kulinonsa.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-11-04.jpg}}

Israeelootu wo’manka coye Maganu hajajinonsa garinni assitu. Hashsu wa’rakkara Maganu Gibitse baalate giddoonni sa”anni umiha baala shii.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-11-05.jpg}}

Israeelootu minna baalate waalchira mundee ganneenna no; kunnira Maganu agure sai. Hatte minna giddo noo manni baalu gati. Israeelootu wilii’lichu mundee widoonni higge gattino.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-11-06.jpg}}

Gibitsootu kayinni Magano diammantino; hajajosino diwonshitino. Kunnira Maganu minnansa agure disaino. Maganu Gibitsootunniha umiha baala shiino.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-11-07.jpg}}

Usuru mine noohunni kayise Ferioooni mine noohu geeshsha mittu mittunku Gibitsennihu bayiru beetti reyino. Manchitu bati’reennansa Gibitsennihu lowo manni kooce wi’li.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-11-08.jpg}}

Hakko hashshanni Feriooni Musenna Aarooni woshshe, <> yii.

//Qullaawu Maxaafi Dhagge:- Fulo 11:1-12:32//
