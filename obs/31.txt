====== 31. Yesuusi Wayi Aanaanni Ha’ri ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-31-01.jpg}}

Yesuusi manna ha’risanni hee’reenna yowolote giddora e’e laaqidhanni widoonni fultanno gede rosaanosira kulinsa. Yesuusi manna ha’risihu gedensaanni huucci’rara ilaala fuli. Hakkiicho Yesuusi callisi no; hashshu taalooti geeshsha huucci’ri.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-31-02.jpg}}

Hatte yannara, rosaanosi yowolo laaqidhanni no; hashshu taalooti geeshsha baaru mereerolla iillitino. Insa widira qole lowo geeshsha bubbisanni noo daafira lowo qarrinni laaqidhanni no.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-31-03.jpg}}

Hakko mereero Yesuusi huuccattosi gude rosaanosiwa ha’ri. Wayi aanaanni ha’ranni baara tayise yowolonniwa mari!

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-31-04.jpg}}

Rosaano Yesuusa affe lowo geeshsha waajjitu; insara ekera affinoha lawinonsa. Yesuusi waajjitinota afe, insa woshshe, <> yiinsa.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-31-05.jpg}}

Hatte yannara Pheexiroosi Yesuusa, <> yii. Yesuusi Pheexiroosi, <> yiisi.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-31-06.jpg}}

Kunnira Pheexiroosi yowolote giddonni fule Yesuusiwa hige qaafi. Ikkollana shiimare qaafihu gedensaanni illesi Yesuusi aananni hooleenna dambala la”anna wolqaataame bubbe macciishsha hanafi.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-31-07.jpg}}

Hatte yannara Pheexiroosi waajjeenna wayi egemma hanafisi. <> yee raari. Yesuusi hakkawoyintenni hiixate angatenni amadisi. Hakkunni gedensaanni Pheexiroosi, <> yiisi.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-31-08.jpg}}

Pheexiroosinna Yesuusi yowolonniwa e’anni heedheennanni bubbe uurriteenna gabbi yii. Rosaanono dhaggeeffatte, <> yite Yesuusira kumbuultusi.

//Qullaawu Maxaafi Dhagge:- Maatewoosi 14:22-23, Maarqoosi 6:45-52, Yohaannisi 6:16-21//
